THE
ESSENTIALS.

01

Writing & Slash Menu

Full live markdown syntax support. Renders as you type. Type / to quickly insert headings, lists, tables, dividers, and media without leaving the keyboard. Drop into raw markdown source mode anytime.

Screenshot: Editor & Slash Menu
02

Organization & Sidebar

Keep things tidy with folders, tags, search, and bookmarks. Multi-select notes for bulk actions. Snap the resizable sidebar fullscreen when needed.

Screenshot: Sidebar & Organization
03

World Mode

A visual canvas for planning: drag out nodes, connect them, arrange your story or project spatially instead of linearly. Fully undo/redo-safe.

Screenshot: World Mode Canvas
04

Provenance ("Mark as")

Select text and honestly mark its source: written by you, by AI, or from another website. Seamlessly integrated into the native context menu.

Screenshot: Provenance Context Menu
05

Format Freedom

Convert your whole workspace (or just one note) between .md, .txt, .html, and .docx in one click. Export to PDF, DOCX, or ODT. Everything is saved as clean, readable files on your disk.

Screenshot: Format & Export
06

Themes & Sync

Dark mode by default, custom frameless title bar. Point your workspace to Google Drive, Dropbox, or Mega to sync through their own client, or sign in with Microsoft for built-in OneDrive sync — no Valx account required, ever.

Screenshot: Settings & Themes
